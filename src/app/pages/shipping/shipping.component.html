Shipping and Delivery Policy

At Dickkny, we strive to provide you with a seamless and efficient shipping and delivery experience. This Shipping and Delivery Policy outlines the terms and conditions related to the shipping and delivery of products purchased from our website. By placing an order with us, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Order Processing

  • Order Confirmation: Once your order is placed, you will receive an order confirmation email with your order details. Please review this information carefully and contact us immediately if any changes are needed.
  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 4 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

2. Shipping Rates and Delivery Estimates

  • Shipping Rates: Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer various shipping options to meet your needs.
  • Delivery Estimates: Delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected. Estimated delivery times will be provided at checkout. Please note that these are estimates and actual delivery times may vary.
    • Standard Shipping: 3 to 4 business days

3. International Shipping

  • Availability: We offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping rates and delivery times for international orders will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
  • Customs, Duties, and Taxes: Your order may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes, which are not included in our shipping charges. These fees are the responsibility of the recipient and are determined by the customs authority of the destination country. Please check with your local customs office for more information.

4. Shipping Confirmation and Order Tracking

  • Shipping Confirmation: You will receive a shipping confirmation email once your order has shipped. This email will include your tracking number and a link to track your order.
  • Order Tracking: You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email. Please allow 24 hours for the tracking information to update.

5. Delivery Issues

  • Incomplete or Incorrect Address: Please ensure that your shipping address is complete and correct. We are not responsible for orders shipped to an incorrect address provided by the customer.
  • Delivery Delays: While we strive to deliver your order within the estimated delivery times, delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, customs processing, or carrier delays. We appreciate your patience and understanding in such cases.
  • Lost or Damaged Packages: If your order is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us immediately at info@dickkny.com with your order number and details. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order.

6. Returns and Exchanges

Please refer to our Return and Exchange Policy for information on how to return or exchange your order.
